 List of Many Projects



KSP 2

2018 - Present

Kerbal Space Program 2 is the title I work on at Star Theory, and is published by Private Division! It's a space sandbox game that teaches you about orbital mechanics and near-future rocket tech. I've worn many hats over the course of this project, from working on startup systems to serialization, the in-game content editor, and more!




DragonDrop

2018

A free block-based educational IDE that was created to make learning programming more accessible! I worked on this as an intern at the DigiPen Research and Development department - the software is based on popular frameworks and tools including Blockly, NodeJS, and Electron, and is used during summer camps and classes at DigiPen!




Bittorio

2016 - 2017

A 3D puzzle fighter made in Unity as an academic project at DigiPen, featuring both single and local multiplayer! This game went to PAX, was nominated for IGF, and is available for download! I Worked on Tools, UI, website/marketing, and did feature prototyping using SocketIO and a custom NGINX and Node.js server. See more on the game's website!
